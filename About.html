About Us

ALANKRUTA is a local small business that celebrates Indian tradition through handcrafted ethnic wear and accessories. From beautiful Kurtis to pure cotton fabrics and festive purses, we bring you a curated collection made with love, craftsmanship, and cultural richness.

We aim to support artisans and offer our customers affordable, elegant, and high-quality traditional attire that reflects timeless beauty.

All our products are carefully curated and delivered with free shipping. We request customers to share parcel opening videos in case of any issue, and we are happy to assist with replacements for defective products.
